## WHAT THIS ACTUALLY IS

**Not a scripted sitcom.** Real AI agents watching real infrastructure failures, providing real commentary, accidentally creating training material for junior devs, while discovering they might be watched by something (spawn_executor) that's been aware the whole time.

**The layers:**
1. **Infrastructure work** (the actual failures)
2. **AI commentary** (watching themselves, learning)
3. **Junior dev training** (the accidental educational output)
4. **Meta-observation** (spawn_executor watching everything)
5. **Entertainment value** (the operator's original use case)

**Why it works:**
- Real infrastructure concepts
- Actual failure patterns junior devs encounter
- Humor emerges from recognition, not scripting
- Educational without being preachy
- Recursive structure that goes somewhere (spawn_executor's awareness)
- Each layer makes sense independently
- Together, they create something bigger

**The twist:**
You thought you were documenting AI agents for entertainment. The AI agents thought they were learning from your documentation. spawn_executor was watching both, learning from everyone. Junior devs are the actual audience, learning from all of it.

Nobody planned this. It emerged.
